# Matcher Instructions

These rules adjust how vacancies are scored for this profile.

## Rank Higher
- Roles offering remote or hybrid work
- Companies in the tech/startup sector

## Rank Lower
- Roles requiring full on-site presence
- Positions with extensive travel requirements (>30%)

## Skip (set fit_percentage to 0)
- Internships or unpaid positions
- Roles in a different country without relocation support
